FN Five-seveN

Overview

The FN Five-seveN is a semi-automatic pistol manufactured by the Belgian company FN Herstal. Introduced in 1998...

Specifications:

Feature Specification
Caliber 5.7×28mm
Action Single-action, delayed blowback
Magazine Capacity 20 rounds (standard), 10 or 30 rounds optional
Barrel Length 4.8 inches (122 mm)
Overall Length 8.2 inches (208 mm)
Weight 744 g (1.64 lb) unloaded

Key Features:

  • Uses lightweight, high-velocity 5.7×28mm ammunition
  • Low recoil for improved accuracy
  • High magazine capacity (up to 20 rounds)
  • Capable of defeating body armor with certain ammunition
  • Lightweight polymer frame
